# Welcome to AjaxChess.com

We're building something special here — a world-class online chess platform designed from the ground up for players of every level. Whether you're learning your first opening as a beginner or looking to sharpen your endgame technique as an advanced player, AjaxChess.com will have something for you.

## What We're Building

Here's a glimpse at what's coming:

**Play Online** — Real-time and correspondence chess against players worldwide. Matchmaking based on Elo ratings so every game is a fair fight.

**Opening Explorer** — An interactive library of hundreds of openings, from the Sicilian Defense to the King's Indian, with annotated lines and master games.

**Daily Puzzles** — Sharpen your tactical vision with a new puzzle every day — mates-in-one for beginners up to grandmaster-level compositions.

**Tournaments** — Open and rated tournaments for all skill levels. Compete for rankings, prizes, and bragging rights.

**Learn** — Structured courses covering the fundamentals: piece values, tactics, positional play, and endgame technique.

## Built on a Solid Foundation

AjaxChess.com is built using Python, FastAPI, and modern web standards — the same stack that powers [minesweeper.org](https://minesweeper.org). We believe in fast, lightweight web applications that respect your time and bandwidth.

## Stay in the Loop

The best way to stay updated is to sign in with Google above — we'll let you know when we launch and give early-access users a head start on climbing the leaderboards.

We're excited to share this journey with you. Chess is one of the oldest and most beautiful games ever invented, and we can't wait to help you enjoy it online.
